About us

CodeCrafters Elite Dev Guild
is a student-led
organization under
ICCT Colleges’ College of Computer Studies that focuses on programming and
software development,
promoting technical skills,
ethics, and teamwork through
workshops, projects, and
competitions.

MISSION

CodeCrafters Elite Dev Guild (CodEv) aims to create a supportive environment where students can learn programming, practice their skills, and collaborate on real projects. Through workshops, coding sessions, and peer learning, the guild helps members grow technically, think creatively, and prepare for future careers in technology.

VISION

To build a strong community of student developers who support each other in learning programming, creating meaningful projects, and growing into confident and responsible technology professionals.

CORE VALUES

CodEv upholds several core values: craftsmanship (clean, maintainable code), excellence (high standards), collaboration (learning together), integrity (ethical computing), innovation (creative solutions), and service (contributing to the community).
